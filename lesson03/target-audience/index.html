Target Audience

This site is for the citizens of Coruscant. There is a wide range of audiences that may visit this site. The most likely visitor will be in the age group (30 - 50) with quite a few teens and yound adults following right behind. These visitors will most likely be people who want to 1. be educated, 2. find fun activities, or 3. follow important events happening today.

Persona 1: Brady
Brady, a high school student, is a citizen of Coruscant and has been asked to do research on his city. He is just wanting simple and interestig facts about the Coruscant that he can use for his assignment.
What Brady will be looking for:

  • Simple facts about Coruscant (when it was founded, major events, weather trends...ect)
  • Since he's in high school, he'd also be intersted in looking at all the activities comming up.

Persona 2: Sarah
Sarah, a 35 year old, is also a citizen of Coruscant and has come to the website for different reasons. She wants to be an informed citizen and wants to look at some of the important events happening in the city at the moment.
What Sarah will be looking for:

  • The most recent Political events that happened in the city.
  • Information about the city (Population, Demographics...ect)
  • Economic status about the city.

Scenarios:

  1. What is the weather like today or what are some of the weather trends like in Coruscant?
  2. What is the biggest event happening right now in Coruscant?
  3. What activities will be happening this upcoming weekend?
  4. What's the latest on the Galactic war?
  5. How many people live in Coruscant?
