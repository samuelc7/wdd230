Point of Coruscant

Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide meaningful information and history about our city, Coruscant. The "Imperial Center" as many call it, is one of the most important cities in the galaxy. This site will help the citizens of our city appreciate our history and facts which we think will help bring unity and keep peace thoughout our land. This site will also be a good resource to important/fun events for individuals and families.
