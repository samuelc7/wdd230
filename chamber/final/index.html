Website Planning Document

Site Purpose

This purpose of this site is to allow customers to learn about the Hotel and become interested enough to book a stay with us. The site will be simple and clear so that each visitor can quickly get a feel for the type of experience they'd have at the hotel. We want all visitors to see that this hotel will be a great fit for those visiting the Washington D.C. temple. Our end goal is to make a descent profit all while providing unforgetable experiences to customers.

Target Audience

The target audience includes those traveling to the Washington D.C. Temple from a great distance. More specifically, those that will need a place to stay that is near the temple. We expect most traffic from people in the 40-65 age group. They are the ones most likely to visit our website and determine if they will stay with us or not. Typical visitor - Dave, 45 years old coming from {state far from Washington D.C.} has a nepheww getting sealed. He and his family needs a place to stay the night after the sealing.
