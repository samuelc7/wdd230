Planet Images

Coruscant, the most populated planet in the Galaxy. Here is the one of the most famous parts of all the Galaxy, the Galactic city.

The City lights of Coruscant. This is why our planet is sometimes called the "Planet that never sleeps."

The Centax-1 Moon overlooks the Galactic City through the night. This is one of four moons of our planet. It is the largest and closest of them all which is why we call it our primary moon.

The Senate Building (Senate Rotunda). This serves as the seat of the Galactic Senate. This is where the major Galactic conferences and meetings take place. There has been more activity here recently due to the ongoing Galactic War.

The Jedi Temple. This is an iconic location here on our planet. This is where the Jedi learn, train, and grow to become Jedi Masters.
